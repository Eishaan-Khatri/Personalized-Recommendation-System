Source

MovieLens 1M public benchmark

The raw data contains anonymous MovieLens users, movie metadata, and timestamped 1-5 star ratings. It is not Pratilipi data and it is not a business-impact dataset.

Evaluation task

Latest positive item holdout

Ratings of 4 or 5 are treated as positive interactions. For each eligible user, the latest positive item is hidden and models try to recover it in the top 10.

Claim boundary

Offline metrics only

Recall, NDCG, coverage, and sample recommendations are real benchmark outputs. Conversion, retention, revenue, and production impact are not claimed.

Measured comparison

Hybrid wins on ranking quality without collapsing coverage.

The top model improves Recall@10 over popularity by 23.3% relative and expands catalog coverage from 2.96% to 15.94%.
