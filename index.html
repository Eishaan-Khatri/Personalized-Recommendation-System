Where the numbers come from

Source

MovieLens 1M public benchmark

The data has anonymous MovieLens users, movie metadata, and timestamped 1-5 star ratings. It is public benchmark data, not Pratilipi data.

Evaluation task

Latest positive item holdout

Ratings of 4 or 5 count as liked items. The second-latest liked item tunes the ranker. The latest liked item is saved for final testing.

Claim boundary

Offline metrics only

Recall, NDCG, coverage, and sample recommendations are real benchmark outputs. Conversion, retention, revenue, and live user impact are not claimed.

Measured comparison

The two-stage ranker finds more hits and uses more of the catalog.

Popularity gets a decent start, but it mostly recommends the same famous items. The hybrid ranker raises Recall@10 from 0.0399 to 0.0584 and coverage from 2.96% to 23.65%.
